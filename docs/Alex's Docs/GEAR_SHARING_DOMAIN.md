### Single Buck
- **Equipment needed**: Crosscut saw, saw handle
- **Personal gear**: crosscut saw, saw handle
- **Shared gear**:
- **Sharing constraint**: If one teammate is using the saw, another cannot use the same saw. College leagues may have one saw for the whole team (school), or may specify how many saws they have for the team. If a school has more than one saw, then two saw teams from the same team/school can be in the same heat. 
- **Partner vs. gear sharing**: This is not a partnered event.
- **Notes**: may be called "saw" or "single saw" in entry form. In the college leagues the saws are owned and shared across a team.

### Double Buck (partnered, same gender)
- **Equipment needed**: Crosscut saw, two saw handles
- **Personal gear**: crosscut saw, two saw handles
- **Shared gear**: 2 people share the saw/handles
- **Sharing constraint**: If one team's partnered pair is using the saw, another team cannot use the same saw. If a school has more than one saw, then two saw teams from the same team/school can be in the same heat. College leagues may have one saw for the whole team (school), or may specify how many saws they have for the team.
- **Partner vs. gear sharing**: This is a partnered event. Competitors on the same team (school)
- **Notes**: may be called "saw" or "double saw" in entry form. In the college leagues the saws are owned and shared across a team

## GENERAL QUESTIONS

### Cross-event gear sharing
> Can the same piece of gear (e.g., one crosscut saw) be shared across multiple events? For example, does one saw get used in Single Buck, Double Buck, AND Jack & Jill?

**Answer:** Yes. Saws are swapped intra-team and intra event especially in the college leagues. Some college teams (schools) may have one saw total, and some schools may have several saws for every event configuration. Same with the pros.
