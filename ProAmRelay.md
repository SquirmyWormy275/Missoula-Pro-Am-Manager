### "Pro-AM" Relay

One of the main purposes of the Missoula Pro Am is to create an environment that breaks down the divide between college athletes and professional athletes. One of the best ways we do this is with a "Pro-Am" relay.

Teams will be randomly drawn prior to the start of the show based on the total number of applicants. Each team consists of exactly 8 competitors:

- 2 Professional Men
- 2 Professional Women
- 2 College Men
- 2 College Women
